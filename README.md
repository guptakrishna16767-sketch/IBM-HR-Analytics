# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | IBM HR Analytics (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset from Kaggle using Power BI. The dashboard helps HR managers understand workforce trends, identify attrition drivers, evaluate employee performance, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze overall workforce composition.
- Identify factors contributing to employee attrition.
- Evaluate employee performance and compensation.
- Provide actionable HR insights and recommendations.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Dataset: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance
- Source: Kaggle
- Total Employees: **1,470**
- Active Employees: **1,233**
- Employees Left: **237**
- Attrition Rate: **16.12%**

## 4️⃣ Key Takeaways & Recommendations

### Key Insights
- Attrition Rate is **16.12%**.
- Employees aged **26–35** have the highest attrition.
- Research & Development has the highest employee exits.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives are the highest-risk job roles.
- Employees with low income and overtime are more likely to leave.

### Recommendations
- Improve retention strategies.
- Reduce overtime.
- Review compensation for lower-income employees.
- Strengthen career development programs.
- Improve employee engagement and work-life balance.
